About Us

The village of Testour is located in the northwest of Tunisia, 77 km southwest of the capital. This village is an icon of the Andalusian passage in the North African region. Indeed, when crossing the village, one feels the Andalusian soul that appears in each square meter traveled, a characteristic architectural style, typical traditional dishes as well as a particular musical style, known by the "Malouf", offering a calm and warm atmosphere delicately accompanied by the sound of the water runoff of Oued de Mejerda. To speak of Testour is also to speak of the Great Mosque and its exceptional clock whose figures are placed upside down and the needles turn counter-clockwise, a phenomenon, until then, unexplained.

Visitors are also invited to visit the famous cave "Ghar Kriz" in Ain Younes which houses a colony of bats, not forgetting to go through the rural cottage "La Verger des Montagnes", a fabulous place to enjoy the enchanting beauty of natural landscapes and a painting in bright colors. Passionate about nature or lovers of discovery, you will undoubtedly be marked by new moments
